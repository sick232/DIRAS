## 1. Strategic Defence Planning Use Case

### Scenario
A defence ministry strategic planner needs to understand all procurement decisions from the last 3 years to inform future procurement policy.

### Current Process (Without DIRAS)
- Manually search through hundreds of documents
- Extract procurement amounts manually
- Aggregate by category and authority
- Create reports (weeks of work)
- Risk of missing documents or inconsistencies

### DIRAS Process
1. **Query**: "All equipment procurement >₹100 crores in last 3 years"
2. **System**:
   - Financial Analysis: Extracts all amounts >₹100 crores
   - Temporal Association: Filters for last 3 years
   - Classification: Identifies procurement documents
   - Aggregation: Groups by equipment type and authority
3. **Output**: Structured report with citations
4. **Time**: Minutes instead of weeks
5. **Quality**: Complete, consistent, auditable

**Benefits**:
- 95% time reduction
- Better decision-making
- Auditability and compliance
- Identifies patterns and trends

## 2. Budget Allocation Optimization Use Case

### Scenario
Finance officer needs to understand current spending patterns and identify optimization opportunities.

### DIRAS Features Used
- Financial Analysis (amount extraction)
- Authority Identification (responsible departments)
- Document Classification (budget vs expenditure)
- Trend Analysis (spending patterns over time)

### Output
- **Spending by Department**: Breakdown of allocations
- **Category-wise Spending**: Capital vs revenue distribution
- **Temporal Trends**: How spending has evolved
- **Anomalies**: Unusual spending patterns
- **Recommendations**: Areas for optimization

**Benefits**:
- Data-driven budget planning
- Identifies redundancies
- Optimizes resource allocation
- Supports evidence-based decisions

## 3. Policy Compliance & Governance Use Case
